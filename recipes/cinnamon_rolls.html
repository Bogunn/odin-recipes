Cinnamon Rolls

Description

Moist and fluffy cinnamon rolls with cane sugar icing and caramel covered apples

  • 2 cups of all-purposeflour
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoons butter, softened (Optional)
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 egg

Filling

  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C)
  2. Whisk flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt together in large bowl
  3. Beat eggs together in another bowl then add to dry ingredients. Mix together with a spatula until soft dough is formed
  4. Work dough on floured surface into 1/4-inch thick rectangle. Brush dough surface with butter.
  5. Whisk 1/2 cup white sugar, brown sugar, and cinnamon together in a small bowl. Sprinkle mixture into the bottom of prepared baking dish.
  6. Sprinkle remainder of cinnamon mixture onto buttered dough. Cut long into 18 and place into prepared dish.
  7. Bake in oven 20-25 minutes
