Eggs

Description

Eggs Benedict with a light drizzle of hollandaise sauce

  • 3 tbs white wine vinegar
  • 4 large free-range eggs
  • 2 toasting muffins
  • 1/2 cup hollandaise sauce
  • 4 slices of parma ham
  1. Bring a saucepan to a boil. Add vinegar to water. Break eggs into ramekins. Cut muffins in half and toast.
  2. Mix water and vinegar to create vortex. It should form round shape. Cook for 2-3 minutes
  3. Repeat process with remain eggs. Place ham on muffins Place eggs on ham. Drizzle hollandaise sauce over top.
