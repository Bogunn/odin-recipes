Pecan Pie

Description

Delicious Southern favorite, Pecan Pie

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups white sugar
  • 1/4 cup dark corn syrup
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 tablespoon cold water
  • 2 teaspoons cornstarch
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/4 cups chopped pecans
  • 1 (9 inch) unbaked pie shell

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F (175 C)
  2. In medium saucepan combine sugar, corn syrup, butter water, and cornstrach. Bring to a full boil and remove from heat
  3. In a large bowl, beat eggs until frothy. Gradually beat in cooked syrup mixture. Stir in salt, vanilla, and pecans. Pour into pie shell.
  4. Bake for 40-45 minutes or until filling sets.
